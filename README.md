# CivicSync 🏛️

**Empowering Citizens, Syncing Cities.**  
CivicSync is a centralized platform designed to bridge the gap between civilians and the Nagar Nigam (Municipal Corporation). It allows citizens to effortlessly report local issues (like potholes, water logging, and broken streetlights) while providing officials with a streamlined dashboard to track, manage, and resolve these issues in real-time.

---

## 📱 How to Use the App (User Guide)

The CivicSync Mobile App is divided into two distinct portals to ensure security and ease of use.

### 1. For Civilians (Citizens) 🙋‍♂️
Citizens can use the app to report problems in their locality and track the progress of municipal work.

**How to Sign Up:**
1. Open the CivicSync app and select the **Citizen Portal**.
2. Click on the **"Register as Civilian"** button.
3. Fill in your details:
   * **Full Name:** Your complete name.
   * **Email Address:** A valid email for notifications.
   * **Password:** Create a strong password.
4. Click **"Create Account"**. You will be instantly registered and logged into the platform.

**How to Login:**
1. If you already have an account, enter your registered **Email Address** and **Password**.
2. Click **"Login to Citizen Portal"**.
*(Note: Your login session is saved securely. You won't have to log in every time you open the app!)*

**What you can do:**
* **Report an Issue:** Click the "+" button to capture a photo of the problem (e.g., a broken road), tag the GPS location, and submit it directly to the Nagar Nigam.
* **Track Status:** View the live status of your reports (Pending, In Progress, Resolved).
* **View Initiatives:** See community updates broadcasted by the municipal corporation.

---

### 2. For Nagar Nigam (Officials) 👮‍♂️
Officials have a restricted, highly secure portal to manage city-wide reports and coordinate repair workflows.

**How to Login:**
Official accounts are pre-configured by the system administrator. Officials cannot "Sign Up" through the app to prevent unauthorized access.
1. Open the CivicSync app and switch to the **Official Portal** tab.
2. Enter your authorized **Agency ID** (e.g., `OFFICIAL123` or your email).
3. Enter your **Secure PIN / Password** (e.g., `123456`).
4. Click **"Login as Official"**.

**What you can do:**
* **Admin Dashboard:** View a master list of all issues reported by citizens across the city.
* **Update Status:** Mark issues as "In Progress" when a team is dispatched, and "Resolved" when the work is complete.
* **Live Map:** View the exact geographical coordinates of reported problems for faster dispatch.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Frontend Mobile:** React Native, Expo, NativeWind (TailwindCSS)
* **Frontend Web:** React, Vite, Framer Motion
* **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB (Mongoose)
* **Cloud Infrastructure:** Vercel (Web), Render (API), Expo EAS (APK Build)
