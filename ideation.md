# Hackathon Ideation 🚀

Here are 5 unique ideas for each of the two problem statements to help us stand out in the hackathon, particularly focusing on "Agentic Depth" and "Usage of Google Technologies" which make up 35% of the evaluation matrix.

---

## Problem Statement 1: The Last-Minute Life Saver

> [!TIP]
> **Evaluation Focus:** Moving beyond traditional reminders to help users take *meaningful action* and make better decisions.

### 1. AI "Intervention" Agent (The Procrastination Buster)
An AI companion that connects with your workspace or browser. When it detects you are nearing a deadline but procrastinating, it intervenes by breaking your daunting task into tiny, 5-minute "micro-tasks" (using Gemini). It lowers the cognitive barrier to starting. 
**Google Tech:** Gemini Pro for task breakdown, Chrome Extension API.

### 2. Context-Aware Dynamic Scheduler
Unlike a static calendar, this app knows how long tasks *actually* take. If you miss a scheduled work block or oversleep, the AI autonomously reshuffles your remaining week's schedule to ensure the deadline is still met without you having to manually drag-and-drop calendar events.
**Google Tech:** Google Calendar API, Google Cloud Functions, Gemini API.

### 3. "Do-It-For-Me" Action Agent
Instead of reminding you to "Pay Bill" or "Reply to Client," the AI connects to your inbox. It drafts the reply or fetches the payment link and sends you a single, actionable message: *"Electricity bill due tomorrow. Reply 'PAY' to execute."* It turns passive reminders into one-click actions.
**Google Tech:** Gmail API, Gemini API for context extraction, Google Cloud Run.

### 4. Gamified "Pomodoro RPG"
Turns deadlines into boss fights. The AI analyzes the complexity of your task and generates a "quest line". Completing sub-tasks deals damage to the boss. If you wait until the last minute, the boss gets a "frenzy" buff, making the task harder. Great for student engagement.
**Google Tech:** Firebase for real-time multiplayer/leaderboards, Gemini API for generating dynamic RPG quests based on real-world tasks.

### 5. Auto-Prep Assistant for Meetings & Interviews
Scans your calendar for upcoming important commitments. 24 hours before an interview or meeting, it autonomously searches the web for the company/attendees, summarizes the agenda, and generates a personalized "prep sheet" with potential questions to ask, ensuring you are never caught off guard.
**Google Tech:** Google Custom Search API, Google Calendar API, Gemini API for summarization.

## Problem Statement 2: Community Hero - Hyperlocal Problem Solver

> [!TIP]
> **Evaluation Focus:** Encourage transparency, accountability, and community participation through intelligent automation.

### 1. AI-Validated Crowdsourced Reporting
Users upload a photo or video of a pothole, garbage dump, or broken streetlight. The AI analyzes the image, identifies the exact issue, assesses severity (e.g., deep pothole vs. shallow), and automatically routes the ticket to the relevant municipal department, filtering out spam.
**Google Tech:** Gemini Pro Vision (for image/video analysis), Google Maps Platform (Geolocation).

### 2. Automated Petition & Follow-up Agent
When a severe issue (like a dangerous intersection) gets enough local upvotes, the platform's AI automatically drafts a formal petition, finds the correct local representative, and sends automated weekly follow-ups (via email or social media) until a response or resolution is received.
**Google Tech:** Gemini API (drafting), Google Cloud Scheduler, Gmail API.

### 3. "Fix-It-Yourself" Micro-Funding
For minor issues (e.g., painting a faded crosswalk, planting a tree), the platform creates a micro-fundraiser. The AI estimates the cost of materials based on the image provided. Once funded by the community, verified local volunteers claim the task, fix it, and upload proof for an automated payout.
**Google Tech:** Gemini Vision (cost estimation), Firebase (database & auth).

### 4. Predictive Infrastructure Dashboard
A dashboard aimed at city officials. It aggregates all citizen reports and uses AI to predict where the next failures will occur. For example: *"Frequent water logging reports in Area X suggest a blocked main drain. High probability of road degradation next week."* 
**Google Tech:** Google Cloud Dataflow, Gemini API (predictive insights), Google Maps JavaScript API for heatmaps.

### 5. Community Bounties & Gamification
A token-based system where citizens earn "Hero Points" for reporting and verifying issues. Local businesses can sponsor these points (e.g., "Get a free coffee at Joe's for 100 points"). The AI cross-references uploaded "fixed" photos with the original report to automatically distribute points.
**Google Tech:** Gemini Vision (verification of before/after photos), Firebase Firestore.
